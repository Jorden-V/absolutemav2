Services

We caiter to all who wish to learn!

Kids Classes

Classes for years 3+. We help your child improve their confidence, discipline and fitness whilst learning self defence in a fun atmosphere.

Adults Classes

Improve your fitness, strength and flexibility whilst learning self defence and a new skill, in a friendly, welcoming environment.

Birthday Parties

Let your child have a fun and structured party with games and activities for all, whilst they learn some martial arts skills.

Holiday Clubs

Struggling to find childcare during the school holidays? We hold camps during most breaks with martial arts and non martial arts activities.

    Kickboxing training is a great way to relieve stress, build confidence and make some great friends. Whilst training in our adults classes, you will improve and gain many new skills such as:

    Coordination
    Flexibility
    Strength
    Self Defence
    Endurance
    Concentration
    Leadership
    Positivity
